The 2nd Imperio tram arrived in Arad

2 July 2020

After the first Imperio tram with number 1907 was delivered to Arad on December 30, 2019, on May 28 a.c. the second tram left the factory, 2008.

Astra Imperio tram number 2008 in the UTA depot. Photo source: MetrouUsor

The lines on which the new Imperio runs and will run

1907 running on line 3. Photo source: MetrouUsor

For the time being, the Imperio trams (1401, 1402, 1403, 1504, 1505, 1506, 1907, 2008) run on line 3, which goes from Fat frumos to Gara Aradul nou. It is also planned that the new trams will run on line 7, which leads to Micalaca.
