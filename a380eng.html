Emirates has withdrawn its first A380!

3 July 2020 The first Emirates A380 withdrawn without the company logo. Photo source: Twitter

The aircraft in question has A6-EDB registration. This aircraft made the last scheduled flight from Muscat to Dubai (DXB) on February 23, 2020, before flying to Dubai World Center on February 28 and returning to Dubai on March 25. He's been there ever since.

Its history - 12 years with Emirates

The A6-EDB was delivered to Emirates in October 2008. It is one of the first A380s to enter service with the airline. Thus, it is not surprising that he is among the first to leave.
