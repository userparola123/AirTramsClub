There are 4 Astra Imperio trams in Oradea

2 July 2020

In 23.05.2020, eBihoreanulhe wrote how the German who was to supervise the tram tests (500 km each on the Oradea lines) was sitting quietly in Germany and waiting for the flights to resume and the quarantine to stop. After only 4 days, AirTramsClub snuck to the fence of the Salca depot trying to immortalize at least one Imperio tram. To our surprise, there were even 2 Imperio trams.

UPTADE: The first tram, 301, was in rehearsals on the night of July 3. You can see the photo here.
