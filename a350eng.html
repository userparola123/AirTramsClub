Premiere: Delta flew an Airbus A350 on the Detroit - Constanța route

12 July 2020

The American company Delta Air Lines operated another flight from the USA to Constanța on Saturday, but this time with Airbus A350-900 (N503DN).

At the beginning of last week Delta made a charter flight on the route Minneapolis - Constanța - NY with Airbus A330. The second flight that the American company is making this year in Romania will be operated with Airbus A350, thus marking a premiere for Constanța, but also for Romania.
