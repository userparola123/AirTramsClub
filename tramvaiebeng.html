STB wants to withdraw the last Czech Tatra trams from the route, after the arrival of the new trams bought by the Capital City Hall. Source: Economica.net

8 July 2020

Almost half a century old, the last Czech Tatra trams still circulating in Bucharest will be taken out of traffic after the arrival of the 100 trams bought by the Capital City Hall, the Bucharest Transporters' Union announces.
